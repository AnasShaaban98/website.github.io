Smart Budget

An Excel-based budgeting tool designed to help users manage their finances effectively.

Designed and built a Smart Budget Excel system to make personal finance management simple and effective. The tool helps individuals and families plan their budgets, track expenses, and stay on top of their financial goals without needing complicated software.

Key Features:
  • Easy Budget Planning: Set your yearly and monthly budgets in a clear, organized layout.
  • Expense Tracking: Record daily spending and instantly see how it affects your budget.
  • Visual Dashboard: Get an at-a-glance view of your finances with charts and summaries that compare planned vs. actual spending.
  • Automatic Updates: Built-in formulas do the math for you â€” no manual calculations required.
  • Customizable Categories: Adjust income and expense categories to fit your personal lifestyle.
Purpose:
This project was created to make budgeting approachable for everyday users. It combines planning, tracking, and insights into one easy-to-use Excel workbook, helping people make smarter financial decisions and build better money habits.
